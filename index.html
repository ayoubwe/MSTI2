Our Playbook

Vision 1

Democratize Access to Robotics & AI

We're making sure youth across all regions can learn, build, and innovate — no matter their background. From rural towns to city centers, everyone deserves the chance to explore tech.

Vision 2

Make Digital Sports a National Priority

Support Morocco's leap into the future with eSports, smart stadiums, VR fan experiences, and athlete performance tech — all while inspiring local talent to lead the charge.

Vision 3

Drive Sports-Tech Innovation

Inspire the next wave of creators to build cutting-edge robotics and AI solutions tailored for the sports industry. Through collaborative spaces with experts, we fuel ideas that transform how we play, train, and engage.

Vision 4

Retain & Empower Local Talent

Let's end the brain drain. By creating meaningful opportunities, we help Moroccan tech minds thrive at home and lead on a global stage.

Vision 5

Prepare for the 2030 World Cup Tech Boom

Position Morocco as a tech pioneer for the World Cup — using smart systems, AR, IoT, and robotics to elevate the fan and athlete experience.

Vision 6

Create a Community of Innovators

Unite students, professionals, and investors around a shared mission to push boundaries in sports and tech. Collaboration is where breakthroughs happen.

Vision 7

Inspire the Next Generation

Through real-world challenges, hands-on learning, and bold goals, we spark curiosity and creativity in young Moroccans — turning passion into purpose.
